List of Subprocessors

30th Jan, 2023
Wcard Technology Private Limited (wCard.io) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users' data. As part of this commitment, we have carefully selected sub-processors to help us provide our services. This page provides information about these subprocessors, their role in our services, and the measures we take to protect user data.

  1. DigitalOcean: DigitalOcean is a cloud infrastructure provider provided by DigitalOcean, LLC. We use DigitalOcean to host our servers and services. This includes storing user data and processing user requests.
  2. Google Analytics: Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze information about how users interact with our website. This information helps us to improve the user experience and make data-driven decisions about our services.
  3. Google Tag Manager: Google Tag Manager is a tag management system provided by Google, Inc. We use Google Tag Manager to manage and implement tracking tags on our website. This allows us to understand user behavior better and improve our services.
  4. Slack: Slack is a team collaboration tool provided by Slack Technologies, Inc. We use Slack to communicate with our team and our users. This includes support requests, bug reports, and other interactions.
  5. Twilio: Twilio is a cloud communications platform provided by Twilio, Inc. We use Twilio to send SMS notifications to our users. This helps us to keep our users informed about important updates and events related to their accounts.
  6. Sendgrid: Sendgrid is an email delivery platform provided by Sendgrid, Inc. We use Sendgrid to send transactional emails to our users, such as account confirmations, password reset notifications, and billing reminders.
  7. Stripe: Stripe is a payment processing platform provided by Stripe, Inc. We use Stripe to process payments from our users for our services. This includes securely storing and processing user payment information, such as credit card numbers.
  8. Razorpay: Razorpay is a payment processing platform provided by Razorpay Software Private Limited. We use Razorpay to process payments from our users for our services in certain regions. This includes securely storing and processing user payment information, such as credit card numbers.
  9. Open AI: OpenAI is an artificial intelligence research lab consisting of the for-profit OpenAI LP and its non-profit parent company, OpenAI Inc.
